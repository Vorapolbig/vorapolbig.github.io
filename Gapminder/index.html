Suicide rate and alcohol consumption

I visualize a bubble chart using alcohol consumption on the y-axis, suicide rate on the x-axis, GDP per capita on the size of the bubbles. Finally, each bubble has a distinct color representing each country. For simplicity of demonstrating the visualization within the report, I only select the Nordic countries (Sweden, Norway, Finland, Denmark). It can be seen that Sweden and Norway share very similarities with both stable suicide rates and alcohol consumption. Denmark shares a similar suicide rate to that of Sweden and Norway but has much higher alcohol consumption. For Finland, the suicide rate has been decreasing with a slight increase in alcohol consumption every year.
